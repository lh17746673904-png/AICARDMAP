# Product Overview

AI Card Map is a public-information research product for AI hardware infrastructure.

The core idea is simple: AI infrastructure is not one company or one product. It is a chain of compute chips, memory, networking, advanced packaging, manufacturing equipment, testing, materials, power, cooling, and deployment systems.

When one link becomes constrained, expensive, hard to qualify, or difficult to scale, it can shape the pace of the whole chain.

## Core Modules

### Industry Map

Shows major AI hardware infrastructure segments and the links inside each segment.

### Link Library

Explains each important link in plain language.

### Company Index

Maps companies to the links where they participate.

### Intelligence Radar

Tracks public signals and places them back into specific value-chain links.

### Learning Paths

Uses text and diagrams to help users understand AI infrastructure without waiting for video lessons.

## Who It Is For

- AI infrastructure researchers
- Industry learners
- Content creators
- Operators who need a structured supply-chain view
- Investors who want industry education, not investment advice

## What It Is Not

AI Card Map is not an investment advisory service. It does not give buy, sell, or hold recommendations.
