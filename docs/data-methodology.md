# Data Methodology

AI Card Map organizes public business information into a value-chain map.

## Source Types

Typical source categories include:

- Company websites
- Product pages
- Annual reports
- Prospectuses
- Filings
- Industry reports
- Supplier disclosures
- Official newsrooms
- Public technical documentation

## Evidence Status

Company and link relationships may be labeled by confidence level.

- Confirmed: supported by official filings, supplier lists, public customer statements, or multiple high-quality sources.
- Public business fit: supported by public product and business positioning, but not necessarily a confirmed customer relationship.
- Needs verification: useful clue, but still requires cross-checking.

## Why This Matters

Supply-chain claims are easy to overstate. A company may have the right capability but not a confirmed customer relationship.

AI Card Map separates business position from customer verification wherever possible.

## Research Boundary

The product is designed for industry research and education. It does not provide investment advice.
