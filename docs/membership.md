# Membership

AI Card Map includes free and member-only areas.

## Free Experience

Free users can experience selected parts of the map and understand how the product works.

## Member Experience

Members can access more complete link profiles, company relationships, intelligence subscriptions, learning paths, and ongoing updates.

## Current Pricing

The overseas version currently supports monthly, quarterly, and annual membership plans.

Prices and benefits may change as the product evolves.

## Important Note

Membership provides access to research organization tools and public-information summaries. It does not include investment advice or personalized recommendations.
